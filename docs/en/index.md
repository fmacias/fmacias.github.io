Senior .NET/C# software developer for backend systems, integration software, and data-driven business applications.

I support companies when existing software must be understood, stabilized, extended, or integrated into real operational environments. My work is especially relevant for grown .NET system landscapes, industrial and MES-related applications, data-processing workflows, and technical interfaces between machines, services, databases, and business systems.

## Profile Summary

I bring more than 20 years of experience in software development, system integration, maintenance, and technical delivery. My strongest area is backend-heavy implementation with `.NET`, `C#`, integration logic, data processing, and reliable delivery inside existing system environments.

I am particularly effective in contexts where software is already used in production, technical debt exists, interfaces must remain reliable, and changes need to be implemented with a clear understanding of operational risk.

- Senior development with strong focus on `.NET`, `C#`, `.NET Framework`, `.NET Standard`, and `.NET Core`
- Experience with MES-related systems, industrial integration, process data collection, and enterprise applications
- Practical strength in SQL, data modeling, query analysis, migrations, and performance improvement
- Experience with integration protocols and interfaces such as `OPC UA`, `SECS/GEM`, `MTConnect`, `MQTT`, `REST`, and `SOAP`
- Confident work in grown codebases, legacy environments, regression testing, QA processes, and release-readiness scenarios
- Additional cross-stack experience with `JavaScript`, `React`, `Vue`, `PHP`, `Magento`, `XML/XSLT`, and selected `Java/Spring` components

## Where I Add Concrete Value

I am not just a feature developer for isolated tickets. My value is strongest where implementation, system understanding, and operational reliability need to come together.

- Further development and stabilization of existing `.NET` and line-of-business applications
- Analysis and improvement of integration flows between machines, gateways, services, databases, and enterprise systems
- Development and maintenance of backend-oriented components for data processing, transformation, import/export, and interface logic
- SQL-focused troubleshooting, query optimization, indexing, migrations, and database-side performance analysis
- Improvement of maintainability, testability, and scalability in grown software systems

## Relevant Working Contexts

My profile fits companies that need experienced technical support in existing system landscapes rather than purely greenfield development.

- Manufacturing execution systems, industrial data collection, and equipment integration
- Enterprise and line-of-business applications with long operational lifecycles
- Backend and middleware systems with a strong integration component
- Database-intensive business systems with performance, migration, or data-quality challenges
- Output management and document-heavy software environments
- E-commerce, PIM, and product-data workflows with complex import and transformation requirements
- Mixed-stack environments where `.NET` is the core, but adjacent technologies must be understood in practice

## How I Work

I work in a structured, direct, and solution-oriented way. In existing systems, the correct answer is rarely to rebuild everything. More often, the relevant work is to understand dependencies, assess technical risk, and implement changes in a way that makes the system more stable, traceable, and maintainable.

- Fast onboarding into existing codebases, data flows, and delivery processes
- Pragmatic modernization instead of unnecessary complete rewrites
- Clear prioritization of stability, maintainability, testability, and operational reliability
- Technical communication at eye level with developers, leads, QA, delivery, and management
- Realistic assessment of effort, risk, and implementation feasibility
