# Services

I do not treat consulting as a fixed product catalog. The relevant question is whether the technical context, delivery need, and operating constraints fit.

## Typical Engagement Shape

- Reinforcing an existing team during further development, stabilization, or modernization
- Taking ownership of a technical area that needs structured senior implementation
- Supporting delivery where integration complexity, legacy constraints, database bottlenecks, or release pressure are slowing progress
- Working across system boundaries when backend, databases, interfaces, data flows, and quality work have to move together

## What Usually Matters

- Clear business or delivery context
- Real technical responsibility, not only coordination language
- Existing systems that need effective progress under practical constraints
- A role where hands-on implementation quality is more useful than additional process overhead
